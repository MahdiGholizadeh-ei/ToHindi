The user interface or UI, which is an abbreviation of the word User Interface, is considered the user's interactive tool with website pages, software, operating system, application, etc. In fact, the audience can communicate with different systems through UI. Today, improving the user interface as much as possible, which ultimately causes user satisfaction, is one of the main topics of IT.
UI design is usually a combination of the following:

visual design; It means the mood and feeling that we are trying to convey

interaction design; That is, the way our design works and its effect on the audience's interaction with the design and content.


Why do we need UI?



It is very clear that everything you present to your audience should be visually attractive and beautiful and pleasing to his taste. A good user interface creates a single visual language that will help improve user interaction with your product. In fact, UI or user interface is the intersection of interaction design, visual design and information architecture, and it will affect all aspects of what you offer to your audience whether you like it or not. . In the absence of a suitable UI, user interaction is not carried out in an optimal way and therefore we will not see ideal results.


What parts does UI design include?


Visual design: The main goal of visual design is to shape and improve the user experience with the help of images, patterns, typography, use of empty spaces, layout and color. Using visual design techniques, you can design the final product based on aesthetic principles and use user-centered designs with the help of well-known rules that have passed their test.

Colors: Due to the unconscious mental associations resulting from the use of different colors and their effect on the emotions and meaning conveyed to the audience, the use of appropriate colors is a necessity for any project. In addition, another important point that should be taken into account is the intelligent use of brand colors according to the intended design and message.

Graphic design: Graphic design is responsible for combining images, typography or moving graphic items or motion graphics in a way that can impress the audience or customer. Graphic design seeks pixel perfection; It means that all points, empty spaces and colors must be in harmony with the principles governing the brand. Note that graphic design is a specialized field and creating extraordinary designs requires mastery and professional skill as well as sufficient experience. In addition, it is often not possible to draw a clear line between graphic design and visual design.

Mockup: A mockup is a smaller or same-size model of the original design that is prepared for preview, design evaluation, advertising, or other purposes. The purpose of mockup design is to show the final appearance of the design with its visual details, such as colors and typography. Mockups are often confused with wireframes and prototyping, but each is actually a different stage of the design process. A wireframe is a less detailed way of presenting a design that shows the structure and outline of the final product. Unlike Wireframe, Mockup is more like a prototype or preview of the final product, but it is not possible to interact with it. With mockup design, you can create a clearer plan of the final UI design in mind and consider more diverse and appropriate options for organizing content.

Typography: Typography is one of the main pillars of most different types of design. Typography is the art and science of using and organizing fonts and its purpose is to present beautiful and legible texts to the reader. In fact, typography adds another layer of meaning to your text.
